The Problem

I recently took a car trip with my family to Ottawa for Canada 150. We drove for many hours along unfamiliar roads in rural areas, and the only places we could stop were the OnRoute gas stations on the highway.

Every so often we would see an exit sign with a fast-food restaurant, exciting! But some times the restaurant would be quite a far detour from the exit, eating fries gets boring after a while. Are there any local eateries on our route?

There is no way of using Google Maps to check a large area for restaurants. I'd have to zoom in on a specific area. That's an issue when I'm not familiar with the region.
